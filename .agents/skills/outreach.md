# Outreach Skill

Guidelines for crafting and sending outreach messages.

## Message Structure (First Contact)
1. **Hook**: Reference something specific about their company or role
2. **Value**: One sentence on what problem you solve
3. **CTA**: A single, low-friction ask (e.g., "Worth a 15-min call?")

## Channel-Specific Rules

### Telegram / WhatsApp
- Keep it under 100 words
- Conversational tone — write like a human, not a template
- No attachments on first message
- Example: "Hi [Name], saw [Company] just launched [thing] — congrats! We help teams like yours [benefit]. Would love to share how. Open to a quick chat?"

### Email
- Subject line: specific and curiosity-driven (not "Partnership opportunity")
- 3–5 short paragraphs max
- One clear CTA at the end
- Plain text preferred over HTML

## Follow-Up Sequence
- **Day 1**: First message
- **Day 4**: Follow-up #1 — add new value or a different angle
- **Day 10**: Follow-up #2 — brief bump, acknowledge it's the last one
- After 3 attempts with no response: mark as unresponsive in CRM
