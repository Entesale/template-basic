# Research Skill

Use computer use (browser) to research companies and contacts before outreach.

## Research Checklist

For each target company:
1. Visit their website — understand what they do, their size, and their target customer
2. Check LinkedIn — find the right contact (title, tenure, recent activity)
3. Look for recent news — funding, product launches, hiring signals, leadership changes
4. Note pain points — what problems do companies like this typically face?

## What to Record
- Company: name, website, industry, estimated size, key product/service
- Contact: full name, title, LinkedIn URL, email (if findable)
- Research notes: anything that makes outreach more personal

## Tips
- Prioritize contacts with decision-making authority (VP, Director, C-suite)
- Recent company news = great conversation starter
- Job postings reveal priorities and pain points
